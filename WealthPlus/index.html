Privacy Policy

Last updated Feb 21, 2022

We firmly believe that your data is yours. Because of this, we never collect any personally identifiable information about anyone using our apps.

What information does Wealth+ collect?

Wealth+ never collects any personal or identifiable information such as transactions, amounts, categories, tags, or any other data you enter within the app.

If you use Google to prefill the profile setup form, your prefill data is still only on your device, but Google might collect your activity while using this feature. Please see their Privacy Policy.

How can I opt out of this data collection?

Well, since we don't collect any data, there isn't any collection to opt-out of! Your input data is stored only on your device. You can remove all input data by yourself by going to Profile > reset and remove all data.

If you use Google to prefill the profile setup form, You can ensure that we cannot access and sneak your data by revoking access by following these instructions.

Changes to this Policy

Changes to this privacy policy may periodically be made and posted here. We suggest you visit this page for updates.
